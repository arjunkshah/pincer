Questions, answered.

Simple answers to common questions about Pincer.

Who is pincer for?
Pincer is built for dyslexic, neurodivergent, and disabled people who want a calmer web.
Does pincer store my data?
No. Pincer does not store your browsing data.
Which sites does pincer work on?
It works on most websites you visit in Chrome.
Do I need a subscription?
The extension is free to install and does not require an account.

Installation

Download the zip and load it as an unpacked extension.

Download

Grab the latest build and keep it on your machine.

Download .zip
Install steps
  1. Unzip the file.
  2. Open chrome://extensions.
  3. Enable Developer mode (top right).
  4. Click “Load unpacked” and select the unzipped folder.
